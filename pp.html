KITTAYYYYY Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters! Here’s what we do and don’t do with your data.

1. Data We Collect

KITTAYYYYY may collect minimal information needed to work on your server (like messages or commands you send to the bot). We do not collect personal info unless you give it to us voluntarily.

2. How We Use Your Data

Data is only used to make KITTAYYYYY function properly. We won’t sell, rent, or share your info with anyone.

3. Data Retention

We keep your data only as long as needed for the bot to work. If you remove the bot, your data is deleted.

4. Third-Party Services

We use third-party database programs to store your data on a private server nobody else can access. Please look into PostgreSQL's Privacy Policy if you need more information. https://www.postgresql.org/about/policies/privacy/

5. Contact
